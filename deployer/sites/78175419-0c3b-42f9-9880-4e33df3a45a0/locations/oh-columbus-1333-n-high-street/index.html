About MilkShake Factory Columbus

At MilkShake Factory our motto is treat yourself to the simple joys in life. We want each visit to start and end with a smile. Our team in Columbus is serving up handspun milkshakes made with homemade ice cream alongside our legacy gourmet chocolate. Enjoy our signature Chocolate Dipped Strawberry milkshake, non-dairy options, chocolate bark varieties and more!
